# Code Of Conduct

Last verified: 2026-03-13

This repository expects respectful, technically rigorous collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and specific without being hostile.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and decisions, not people.
- Share context that helps others reproduce problems and review changes.
- Respect security boundaries, private disclosures, and maintainer requests during incident handling.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or security-sensitive details without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that blocks productive review or collaboration.
- Deliberately bypassing repository safeguards, approval gates, or audit requirements.

## Reporting

Use the repository's security reporting guidance for vulnerabilities. For conduct issues, contact the maintainers through a private channel rather than escalating publicly in issue threads.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.
